Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a culinary recipe that involves layers of pasta and fillings.

There is a huge variety of filling combinations for lasagna, the best known of which uses tomato sauce and cheese in addition to layers of pasta.

Lasagna can usually be found in supermarkets, so it is extremely easy to make this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Supermarket Lasagna

Steps

  1. Open the lasagna package
  2. Put the lasagna in the microwave
  3. Set a time of 3 minutes and wait
  4. Remove the lasagna from the microwave
  5. Serve on a suitable plate
