Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a classic food consisting of an open crust with a layer of tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, topped with various ingredients.

In this recipe, we will learn how to make an authentic pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarela Cheese
  • Slices of Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Place the dough on top of a platform
  2. Add a layer of tomato sauce that covers the entire surface of the pizza
  3. Add grated mozzarela on top of the tomato sauce
  4. Distribute slices of pepperoni across the entire pizza
  5. Bake in the oven for 35 minutes
  6. Remove from the oven and serve the pizza in a suitable container
